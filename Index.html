ABOUT BLOCSOC
We are a group of Blockchain enthusiasts aiming to unite the blockchain community of IIT Roorkee, in a highly collaborative environment that is open for all. We are always open to collaborations on fun projects, and quirky discussions on crypto, Defi, Web3.0 and beyond.
An Open Community to increase interaction among Blockchain enthusiasts and bitcoin-dev enthusiasts and to spread the word about 'Web 3.0'
AIM OF THE HACKATHON
0xStackTooDeep is a 3 days long hackathon organized by BlocSoc(Sponsored by GWEI & WEI) with the goal of inspiring and fostering a community of builders promoting technological advancements in the Web3 space with a special focus on Web3 security. Packed with amazing sessions by industry experts and workshops, we invite you to grab the opportunity to interact and learn from the leading minds in the ecosystem and build secure and innovative decentralized applications for the future
